# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant v2.1.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include demonstrating empathy and kindness, being respectful of differing opinions, giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback, and focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include personal attacks, trolling, public or private harassment, publishing others' private information without permission, and any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Reports may be sent privately to the maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
